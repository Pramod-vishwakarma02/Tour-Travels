About Kedarnath

**Kedarnath**, nestled in the majestic **Garhwal Himalayas** of Uttarakhand, is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in India. It is home to the **Kedarnath Temple**, dedicated to Lord Shiva and one of the twelve **Jyotirlingas**.

Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and serene natural beauty, the trek to Kedarnath is as spiritually rewarding as it is visually breathtaking. Devotees and trekkers alike come from around the world to experience this divine and tranquil destination.
