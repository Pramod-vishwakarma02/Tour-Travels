About Amritsar

**Amritsar**, located in the northwestern state of Punjab, is a city rich in history, devotion, and culture. It is best known for the stunning **Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)** — the holiest site in Sikhism, where peace and spirituality meet in perfect harmony.

Beyond the Golden Temple, Amritsar also houses the historic **Jallianwala Bagh**, and hosts the emotional **Wagah Border ceremony**, offering visitors a powerful experience of India’s heritage and patriotism. Don't miss the vibrant local bazaars and the famous **Amritsari cuisine**!
