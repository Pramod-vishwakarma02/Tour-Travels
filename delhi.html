About Delhi

**Delhi**, the capital of India, is a city where ancient history and vibrant modern life coexist. From grand Mughal-era monuments like the **Red Fort** and **Qutub Minar** to bustling markets like **Chandni Chowk**, Delhi offers a rich cultural experience.

The city is also known for its government institutions, modern infrastructure, diverse cuisine, and world-class museums. Whether you're exploring **India Gate**, enjoying the serenity of **Lotus Temple**, or shopping in **Connaught Place**, Delhi has something for every traveler.
