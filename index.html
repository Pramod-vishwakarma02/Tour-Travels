Historic Places

Golden temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is the most sacred shrine of Sikhism, located in Amritsar, Punjab. It is renowned for its stunning gold-covered structure and its serene setting in the middle of a sacred pool, known as the Amrit Sarovar.

Krdarnath

Kedarnath is a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site located in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, India. It is home to the ancient Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India.

ladakh

Ladakh, often called the “Land of High Passes,” is a breathtaking region in northern India, nestled between the Himalayas and the Karakoram ranges.
