Jaypur,Hawa Mahal

Rajasthan, is a stunning five-story palace made of red and pink sandstone. Known as the "Palace of Winds," it was built to allow royal women to observe street festivities without being seen.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, located in Karnataka, is a grand royal residence known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture and intricate interiors. It is beautifully illuminated at night and is a major tourist attraction, especially during the Dussehra festival.
